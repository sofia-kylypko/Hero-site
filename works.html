SOME MY FAVOURITE WORKS OF MIYAZAKI AND STUDIO GHIBLI

THE TALE OF THE PRINCESS KAGUYA
2013

A tiny nymph discovered inside a bamboo stalk develops into a gorgeous and attractive young woman who gives her admirers a series of nearly difficult jobs to complete in order to show their love for her.

SPIRITED AWAY
2001

Chihiro and her parents stumble upon an amusement park with a stall offering a variety of cuisine after taking a mistaken turn down a forested trail. She is shocked to see Chihiro's parents start to consume before changing into swine. While waiting for a reconciliation with her parents in this supernatural world, Chihiro meets a variety of personalities and works in a bathhouse for ghosts.

MY NEIGHBOR TOTORO
1988

Schoolgirl Satsuke and her sister Mei move into an old country home with their father and wait for their mother to recuperate from a sickness in a nearby hospital. The sisters meet and make friends with playful spirits as they investigate their new house and the surrounding woodland, most notably Totoro, a huge, cuddly creature.

PRINCESS MONONOKE
1997

The conflict between a woodland princess and the advance of automation involves a prince.

NAUSICAÄ OF THE VALLEY OF THE WIND
1984

In a distant future, after an apocalyptic war has largely destroyed the ecology of the world, the few remaining humans reside in hospitable areas within what has turned into a "toxic jungle." Young Nausicaä is able to converse with the enormous insects that live in the hazardous forest and resides in the dry Valley of the Wind.

THE WIND RISES
2013

Jiro Horikoshi, a Japanese aircraft engineer whose illustrious career includes the development of the A-6M World War II fighter jet, has been inspired by flying his entire life.

HOWL'S MOVING CASTLE
2004

At hat store, Sophie leads a routine existence until she makes friends with the magician Howl, who resides in a fantastic floating castle. The wicked Witch of Waste uses a spell on young Sophie to unnaturally age her. To defeat the envious hag and bring Sophie back to her previous vitality and attractiveness, Howl must now employ all of his magical skills.

CASTLE IN THE SKY
1986

A group of air pirates headed by the matronly Dola assault the aircraft carrying the orphan Sheeta and her abductor, Col. Sheeta finds fellow orphan Pazu after using a magic crystal around her neck to avoid a midair collision. The two work together to explore the magical floating city of Laputa while being chased by both Muska and pirates who are after the city's wealth of riches.
